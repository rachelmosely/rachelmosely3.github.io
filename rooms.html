Accommodations

The Arbor offers four types of accommodations for groups ranging from one to four guests: king rooms, double rooms, junior suites, and Arbor suites. All rooms offer en suite baths, private outdoor space, and other amenities listed below.

All rooms feature:

  • A private terrace and/or patio
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi
  • On-demand films, TV, and music
  • Egyptian cotton bed linens
  • Complimentary Kiehl's bath products
  • Steam showers
  • Full-service minibar
  • 24/7 room service

King

450 square foot guest room featuring one California king-size bed and one en suite bath.

Double

550 square foot guest room featuring two queen-size beds and one en suite bath.

Junior Suite

800 square foot suite featuring one bedroom and large sitting room.

Arbor Suite

1250 square foot suite featuring two bedrooms and large sitting room.
